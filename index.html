🎯 Project Overview
A modern connection platform that combines personality-based matching, video profiles, and AI-enhanced features to create meaningful connections. Choose your mode: Find Your Vibe (friendships), Find Your Heart (dating), or Find Both (open to all connections).
  • • 🌟 Dual-mode platform: Friends (Find Your Vibe) & Dating (Plenty of Hearts)
  • • 🧠 Myers-Briggs inspired personality assessment
  • • 📹 Short-form video profiles (15-30 seconds)
  • • 🤖 AI-powered match insights and conversation starters
  • • 💬 Smart messaging with icebreaker suggestions
  • • 🏘️ Interest-based communities with events and discussions
  • • 🎯 Enhanced matching through shared community activities
  • • 💎 Premium features and subscription tiers
