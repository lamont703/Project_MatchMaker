================================================================================
                              DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

🎨 VISUAL HIERARCHY:
- Large, prominent CTAs with gradient backgrounds
- Clear iconography for quick recognition
- Video-first approach with prominent video areas
- Personality badges as key differentiators

💻 INTERACTION PATTERNS:
- Click & drag or button clicks for primary actions
- Click for secondary actions and details
- Hover states for enhanced desktop experience
- Right-click for additional options (desktop)
- Touch gestures maintained for mobile responsiveness

🌈 COLOR SYSTEM:
- Primary: Purple-to-pink gradient (#8B5CF6 → #EC4899)
- Secondary: Warm orange (#F97316)
- Success: Green (#10B981)
- Warning: Red (#EF4444)
- Background: White/light gray (#FFFFFF, #F9FAFB)

🤖 AI INTEGRATION POINTS:
- Match percentage calculations
- "Why We Match" explanations
- Icebreaker suggestions
- Conversation tips and prompts
- Profile optimization recommendations

⚡ KEY FEATURES:
- 15-30 second video profiles
- Personality-based matching (6 types)
- AI-powered insights and suggestions
- Game-like drag/click interface with responsive design
- Premium features for enhanced experience
